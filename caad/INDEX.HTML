About Us

Welcome to LEARNING HUB Institute, a pioneering educational platform that empowers individuals to excel in the rapidly evolving world of Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and Drafting. With a specialized focus on industries such as civil engineering, mechanical design, architecture, and information technology, we are committed to providing cutting-edge training and industry-relevant skills to the next generation of professionals. At LEARNING HUB, we blend practical, hands-on experience with theoretical knowledge, equipping students with the tools needed to thrive in their respective fields. Our expert instructors bring years of real-world experience to the classroom, ensuring that every lesson is rooted in the latest industry trends and technological advancements. We offer a wide range of courses tailored to meet the needs of aspiring engineers, architects, and IT professionals. Whether you're looking to master AutoCAD, Revit, SolidWorks, or other leading CAD software, LEARNING HUB ensures you receive top-tier education that opens doors to limitless career opportunities. Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and industry by nurturing talent, fostering innovation, and shaping the future of design and drafting professionals. Through our dedicated approach and state-of-the-art resources, we strive to create an environment that promotes learning, growth, and success. Join us at LEARNING HUB Institute—where technology meets education to design the future.

Our Highlighted Courses

Web Development

Our Web Development course equips you with the skills to build modern, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular frameworks like React and Angular.

  • Learn front-end and back-end development
  • Understand user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design
  • Build real-world projects

DATA ANALYSIS

Our Data Analyst course is designed to equip you with the skills needed to interpret and analyze data effectively. You will gain expertise in data collection, statistical analysis, and reporting using tools such as Excel, SQL, and Tableau. Master the techniques to turn raw data into actionable insights.

  • Learn data collection, organization, and cleaning techniques
  • Master statistical analysis and data interpretation
  • Gain proficiency in data visualization tools and reporting techniques

SOLIDWORKS

Our Solidworks course is designed to provide hands-on training in 3D mechanical design and modeling. Learn how to create precise engineering models, assemblies, and simulations to bring your mechanical designs to life.

  • Master 3D modeling and assembly creation
  • Develop skills in simulation and analysis
  • Learn how to create technical drawings and documentation

AUTO CAD

Our AutoCAD course focuses on the essential skills required to design and draft detailed architectural and civil engineering plans. Learn to create accurate 2D and 3D drawings used in construction and infrastructure projects.

  • Develop proficiency in creating 2D floor plans and layouts
  • Master 3D modeling for civil engineering projects
  • Learn how to produce detailed construction documentation and blueprints

Max-For-Engineers

Our Max for Engineers course is designed to provide professionals with the skills needed to master 3D modeling and rendering using Autodesk 3ds Max. This course covers the essentials of creating realistic visualizations for engineering projects, including architectural design, infrastructure modeling, and product development.

  • Learn to create highly detailed 3D models for engineering and architectural projects
  • Master advanced rendering techniques for realistic visualizations
  • Understand how to create virtual walkthroughs and presentations for engineering proposals

Revit-Architecture

Our Revit Architecture course equips architects and designers with the skills needed to create intelligent 3D models for building design and construction. Learn how to use Revit’s powerful tools to improve the efficiency of your design workflow, collaborate on multidisciplinary teams, and produce detailed architectural documentation.

  • Master the creation of detailed 3D models for building design and construction
  • Learn how to produce accurate architectural drawings, floor plans, and sections
  • Understand how to collaborate seamlessly with engineers and other project stakeholders

PDMS Software

Our PDMS (Plant Design Management System) course is tailored for engineers and designers working in the oil, gas, and petrochemical industries. Learn how to design and model complex industrial plants using PDMS’s powerful 3D design and visualization tools. This course also covers the integration of various engineering disciplines within PDMS for efficient project management.

  • Develop expertise in creating 3D plant models for industrial facilities
  • Learn how to perform clash detection and optimize plant designs
  • Understand how to generate construction drawings and manage plant design data

NX CAD

Our NX CAD course provides comprehensive training in Siemens NX, a leading software for product design, engineering, and manufacturing. This course covers advanced techniques in 3D modeling, assembly design, and simulation, aimed at professionals in the automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors.

  • Master the creation of 3D models and complex assemblies
  • Learn to simulate product behavior with advanced CAE tools
  • Understand how to streamline the design-to-manufacturing process using NX CAD

Mastercam

Our Mastercam course offers in-depth training in Mastercam, a leading software for CNC programming and machining. This course is designed for engineers and machinists who want to master the tools for creating precise toolpaths, optimizing machining processes, and improving production efficiency in manufacturing industries.

  • Learn to create 2D and 3D toolpaths for CNC machines
  • Understand how to program multi-axis CNC machines for complex parts
  • Gain proficiency in optimizing machining operations for faster and more efficient production

Ansys

Our Ansys course provides comprehensive training in Ansys simulation software, used by engineers and designers to perform finite element analysis (FEA), computational fluid dynamics (CFD), and other advanced simulations. This course is designed to help professionals solve complex engineering problems and optimize product performance across various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.

  • Learn to perform structural, thermal, and fluid flow simulations
  • Understand how to use Ansys for design optimization and product validation
  • Gain expertise in interpreting simulation results and making data-driven design decisions

Creo

Our Creo course offers in-depth training in Creo, a powerful 3D CAD software developed by PTC. This course covers advanced modeling techniques, assembly design, and parametric design, helping engineers and designers create complex and precise product designs. Whether you're in the automotive, aerospace, or consumer product industries, Creo is the tool that brings your ideas to life.

  • Master 3D parametric modeling for product design and development
  • Learn to create and manage assemblies for complex engineering projects
  • Gain proficiency in advanced surfacing, sheet metal design, and more

Catia

Our Catia course provides specialized training in Catia, one of the most advanced CAD software used for product design, engineering, and manufacturing. This course focuses on mastering 3D modeling, surface design, and assembly creation, empowering professionals in industries like aerospace, automotive, and industrial equipment to create innovative and highly detailed products.

  • Learn to create complex 3D models and assemblies using advanced CAD tools
  • Master surface modeling and sheet metal design techniques
  • Gain expertise in product lifecycle management (PLM) and integration with other engineering tools

5D BIM with Navisworks

Our 5D BIM with Navisworks course focuses on leveraging the power of Autodesk Navisworks to manage and visualize construction projects through 3D and 4D simulations, with an added focus on 5D for cost management. This course equips professionals with the skills needed to improve project coordination, detect clashes, and enhance the management of time, cost, and resources in construction and infrastructure projects.

  • Learn to integrate 3D models with time schedules (4D) and cost data (5D)
  • Master clash detection and coordination in multi-disciplinary projects
  • Gain proficiency in project visualization, scheduling, and resource management using Navisworks
