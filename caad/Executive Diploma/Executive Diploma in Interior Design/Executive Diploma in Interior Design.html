Executive Diploma in Interior Design

Explore the Art of Interior Design: Course Overview

The Executive Diploma in Interior Design is an innovative program tailored for creative minds passionate about transforming spaces. This course blends design theory, practical application, and modern tools to craft functional and aesthetically pleasing interiors.

Participants will gain expertise in creating unique designs that meet client requirements while integrating sustainable practices, space planning, and cutting-edge technology.

Why Choose This Course? Key Features

  • Hands-on training in industry-standard software such as AutoCAD, SketchUp, and Adobe Suite
  • Live project opportunities in residential, commercial, and institutional interiors
  • Guidance from award-winning interior designers
  • Emphasis on creativity, innovation, and client-centric designs
  • Integration of sustainable design practices

Master Interior Design Skills: Detailed Course Modules

  • Fundamentals of Interior Design: Concepts, principles, and elements of design
  • Space Planning and Furniture Layout: Creating functional designs
  • Materials and Finishes: Choosing the right textures and colors
  • Lighting Design: Enhancing ambiance and functionality
  • Sustainable Design: Eco-friendly practices and material selection

Bright Career Opportunities: Placements

Graduates of this program are highly valued in the design industry. Our placement team ensures you are well-prepared for success in various interior design roles.

  • Top recruiters include Gensler, HOK, Perkins+Will, and Stantec.
  • Job roles such as Interior Designer, Space Planner, and Color Consultant await you.

Future-Ready Careers: Scope of Interior Design

The demand for creative and sustainable design solutions is on the rise. Upon completing this course, you can:

  • Establish yourself as a Freelance Designer or Consultant.
  • Work with global architecture and design firms.
  • Pursue advanced studies or certifications in specialized design areas.

Flexible Learning: Course Duration & Format

This 12-month program offers flexible learning options designed for professionals:

  • Evening and weekend classes available.
  • Choose between online and in-person sessions.
